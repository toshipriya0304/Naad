    About NAAD

    Music! Its feet stead fasted in the deepest of the seas and its head held high into the sky! Indeed, it’s something so pure, so serene! The melody of the primordial sound that leaves us enchanted, the sound of the cosmos and of human consciousness, a sound that transcends space and time, a sound that has no beginning or end, a sound that isn’t simply music, a sound that’s magic- NAAD!

    Gear up folks, as Dhwani- Music Club, BIT Mesra returns with its annual music fest- NAAD! The wait is finally over and the excitement is going to take you on a roll! Get ready to witness music in its purest form through a series of electrifying events and competitions ranging from Hard-core metal rock to spellbinding tunes of Indian Classical Music! Scintillating nights and exhilarating events await!

    So prepare yourselves to hit the right notes, knock up your instruments to strike the right chords and get ready to flow in a mesmerizing musical journey with us!

    Swaranjali

    Witness the elating spectacle of raagas and lose yourselves in the prosperous heritage of Indian classical music with Swaranjali- the Classical Singing competition!

    RULES FOR SWARANJALI
    1. Only pure classical compositions are allowed.
    2. Participants must perform solo with a maximum of 2 accompanying instrumentalists.
    3. No accompanying instruments are allowed except a harmonium, tanpura and tabla. Participants may bring their own instruments. A harmonium and a table set will be provided.
    4. Time limit is 10 minutes (Stage in to Stage out) and it should be strictly followed. Participants will be heavily penalized by the judges for not doing so. Negative marking will be done.
    5. The decision of the judges shall be final and binding.


    Click on Swaranjali to register for this event.






    Tarang

    The mellifluous notations of the keyboard, the divine strings of the sitar or the rocking rhythm of the guitar- immerse yourselves in the aura of euphony as NAAD presents to you the instrumental competition- Tarang!

    RULES FOR TARANG
    1. Pure instrumental performance pertaining to any genre is allowed.
    2. Participants can participate solo or in groups of 2-7 members.
    3. All kinds of instruments are allowed. Participants have to bring their own instruments. Only a standard drum-kit will be provided.
    4. Participants cannot use a backing track.
    5. The time limit is 3-7 minutes (stage in- stage out). Negative marking will be done for participants who exceed the time limit.
    6. The decisions taken by the judges will be final and binding.


    Click on Tarang to register for this event.






    Jhankaar

    With the peppy Bollywood music and the soulful Hindi songs, witness the symphonious splendour of Hindi music with Jhankaar- the Eastern Singing competition!

    RULES FOR JHANKAAR
    1. Only decent Bollywood songs and songs of Hindi language are allowed.
    2. Participants can bring their own track or perform with accompanying instrumentalists (if any).
    3. Participants can either perform solo or in groups of 2-7 (including instrumentalists).
    4. Time limit is 3-7 minutes (Stage in to Stage out) and it should be strictly followed. Participants will be heavily penalized by the judges for not doing so. Negative marking will be done.
    5. Obscenity (at the discretion of judges) of any kind in the lyrical content of the song is not allowed and will lead to immediate disqualification.
    6. The decision of the judges shall be final and binding.


    Click on Jhankaar to register for this event.






    Surreal Symphony

    Get ready to strike the chords of euphoria and flow in with the ever- dynamic opulence of western music as NAAD presents to you Surreal Symphony- the western singing competition!

    RULES FOR SURREAL SYMPHONY
    1. Only decent English songs are allowed.
    2. Participants can bring their own track or perform with accompanying instrumentalists (if any).
    3. Participants can either perform solo or in groups of 2-7 (including instrumentalists).
    4. Time limit is 3-7 minutes (Stage in to Stage out) and it should be strictly followed. Participants will be heavily penalized by the judges for not doing so. Negative marking will be done.
    5. Obscenity (at the discretion of judges) of any kind in the lyrical content of the song is not allowed and will lead to immediate disqualification.
    6. The decision of the judges shall be final and binding.


    Click on Surreal Symphony to register for this event.






    Maati

    Gear up to flow in with the perfect blend of ritualistic folk and cultural expressions of music as NAAD brings to you the folk/regional singing competition- Maati!

    RULES FOR MAATI
    1. Only regional/folk songs are allowed.
    2. The time limit is 3-7 minutes. Negative marking will be done on exceeding the time limit.
    3. Both solo and group performances are allowed.
    4. There can be 2-7 members in a group (including instrumentalists).
    5. There should not be any kind of obscenity in the lyrics.
    6. The decision of the judges shall be final and binding.
